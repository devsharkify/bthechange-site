OBJECTIVE

What we set out to do.

Provide legal and humanitarian support to the Indian diaspora.

CONTEXT

Why now.

The large Indian diaspora, particularly students and NRIs, often faces unique challenges abroad related to immigration, family, and legal matters. This project acts as a vital support system, providing guidance and emergency assistance to those far from home.

KEY ACTIVITIES

What we actually do.

  1. Offering guidance on family, immigration, and legal issues in the UK.
  2. Providing emergency support through crowdfunding, repatriation, and medical aid.
  3. Assisting students with finding part-time jobs and suitable accommodation abroad.
IMPACT

The result.

Active since 2014, the project has supported dozens of families and students, fostering a sense of security and community for Indians abroad.
