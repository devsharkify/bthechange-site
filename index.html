FOUNDER'S NOTE · RAM CHALLA

It started with one question.

Ram Challa, founder of B The Change Welfare Society, has spent two decades doing one thing extraordinarily well: showing up. A practising lawyer and UK Solicitor, he has filed PILs that protected forests, built classrooms that taught a generation Python, and stood with families across India and the UK. Know work →

WORKING PRESIDENT

Every program runs through young people.

Rohan leads the Youth & Education portfolio across all nine programs. An MBA from IIM Kozhikode and a law graduate from NALSAR Hyderabad, he has spent the last decade in public service, mostly inside classrooms, panchayat halls, and the back rooms of state-level education reform. Know work →
